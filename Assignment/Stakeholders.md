<h1>Stakeholder Register for InDrive Clone</h1>
<br>
<h2>Introduction</h2>
This document provides a detailed Stakeholder Register for the InDrive Clone project, listing the various stakeholders involved in its development and operation. Each stakeholder is essential to the platform's success, and understanding their interests, roles, and influence ensures the project meets its objectives. By classifying stakeholders into internal and external groups, we aim to foster communication and collaboration, enabling a seamless experience for users and stakeholders alike.
<hr></hr>
<h2>Internal Stakeholders</h2>
<h2>1. Software Development Team</h2>

Role: Build and maintain the core platform.
Responsibilities:
Develop new features such as ride-hailing and driver management.
Maintain code quality and ensure system optimization.
Fix bugs and resolve technical issues. <br><br>
Influence: High <br>
Interest: High <br>
Category: Internal <br>
<h2>2. Product Managers</h2>

Role: Define the product vision and manage the project roadmap.
Responsibilities:
Prioritize features based on user needs.
Align platform development with business goals.
Communicate key requirements to the development team. <br><br>
Influence: High <br>
Interest: High <br>
Category: Internal <br>
<h2>3. UI/UX Designers</h2>

Role: Design user-friendly interfaces for both drivers and riders.
Responsibilities:
Design intuitive booking and driver management screens.
Optimize user experience for mobile platforms.
Conduct usability testing and user research. <br><br>
Influence: Medium <br>
Interest: High <br>
Category: Internal <br> 
<h2>4. Quality Assurance (QA) Team</h2>

Role: Test platform functionality and performance.
Responsibilities:
Conduct automated and manual testing for all features.
Report and track bugs.
Perform regression testing after updates. <br><br>
Influence: Medium <br>
Interest: High <br>
Category: Internal <br>
<h2>5. DevOps Engineers</h2>

Role: Manage cloud infrastructure and deployment pipelines.
Responsibilities:
Ensure uptime and scalability during peak usage.
Automate deployment processes and continuous integration.
Manage system reliability. <br><br>
Influence: High <br>
Interest: Medium <br>
Category: Internal <br>
<h2>6. Data Analysts and Scientists</h2>

Role: Analyze driver and rider data for insights and platform optimization.
Responsibilities:
Generate insights for personalized ride recommendations and price optimization.
Support decision-making with data-driven insights. <br><br>
Influence: Medium <br>
Interest: High <br>
Category: Internal <br>
<h2>7. Cybersecurity Team</h2>

Role: Ensure the platform's security and protect user data.
Responsibilities:
Implement measures to protect against hacking and data breaches.
Monitor for vulnerabilities and manage responses to security threats.
Ensure compliance with data protection regulations. <br><br>
Influence: High <br>
Interest: Medium <br>
Category: Internal <br>
<h2>8. System Architects</h2>

Role: Design the overall architecture for the platform.
Responsibilities:
Ensure system scalability and flexibility.
Choose appropriate technologies and frameworks.
Define performance benchmarks. <br><br>
Influence: High <br>
Interest: Medium <br>
Category: Internal <br>
<h2>9. Customer Support Team</h2>

Role: Provide support to both drivers and riders.
Responsibilities:
Handle complaints and technical issues.
Troubleshoot problems related to bookings, payments, and cancellations.
Escalate complex issues to relevant teams. <br><br>
Influence: Medium <br>
Interest: High <br>
Category: Internal <br>
<h2>10. Partnership Managers</h2>

Role: Establish partnerships with fuel stations, service centers, and vehicle providers.
Responsibilities:
Build partnerships to offer benefits to drivers (e.g., discounts on fuel, maintenance).
Manage contracts and negotiate terms. <br><br>
Influence: Medium <br>
Interest: High <br>
Category: Internal <br>
<h2>11. Marketing and Growth Team</h2>

Role: Promote the platform and acquire new drivers and riders.
Responsibilities:
Run campaigns targeting driver recruitment and rider growth.
Monitor key metrics like user retention and engagement.
Optimize marketing strategies based on campaign performance. <br><br>
Influence: High <br>
Interest: High <br>
Category: Internal <br>
<h2>12. Legal and Compliance Team</h2>

Role: Ensure platform compliance with transport and data privacy laws.
Responsibilities:
Review contracts and partnership agreements.
Handle legal disputes and driver or rider complaints.
Ensure adherence to regulations like GDPR and transport laws. <br><br>
Influence: High <br>
Interest: Medium <br>
Category: Internal <br>
<h2>13. Accessibility Team</h2> 

Role: Ensure the platform is accessible to all users, including those with disabilities.
Responsibilities:
Implement accessibility features like voice commands.
Ensure compliance with accessibility guidelines (e.g., ADA).
Test the platform for accessibility barriers. <br><br>
Influence: Medium <br>
Interest: High <br>
Category: Internal <br>
<h2>14. Data Privacy Officer</h2>

Role: Oversee user data handling and ensure compliance with data privacy regulations.
Responsibilities:
Conduct regular audits of data handling practices.
Ensure compliance with regulations like GDPR and CCPA.
Handle user requests related to data rights. <br><br>
Influence: High <br>
Interest: Medium <br>
Category: Internal <br>
<hr></hr>
<h2>External Stakeholders</h2>
<hr></hr>
<h2>1. API Integration Partners</h2>

Role: Provide external services like payment gateways or maps.
Responsibilities:
Manage API integration for payment and location services.
Ensure smooth communication between external services and the platform. <br><br>
Influence: Medium <br>
Interest: Medium <br>
Category: External <br>
<h2>2. Drivers</h2>

Role: Provide ride services to the users of the platform.
Responsibilities:
Use the platform to accept and complete ride requests.
Maintain a high level of service and customer satisfaction.
Provide feedback on platform features and usability. <br><br>
Influence: High <br>
Interest: High <br>
Category: External <br>
<h2>3. Riders</h2>

Role: End-users who book rides through the platform.
Responsibilities:
Use the platform to book and pay for rides.
Provide feedback and report issues to enhance the user experience. <br><br>
Influence: High <br>
Interest: High <br>
Category: External <br>
<h2>4. Regulators</h2>

Role: Ensure compliance with transportation and data privacy laws.
Responsibilities:
Monitor the platformâ€™s adherence to local transport regulations.
Oversee data protection and payment processing.
Ensure compliance with consumer protection standards. <br><br>
Influence: High <br>
Interest: Medium <br>
Category: External <br>
<h2>5. Advertisers and Sponsors</h2>

Role: Promote their products and services on the platform.
Responsibilities:
Run ads targeting riders and drivers on the platform.
Provide promotional materials for campaigns.
<br><br>
Influence: Medium <br>
Interest: Medium <br>
Category: External
